About Us
Team Drishti
REVINER

Drishti is the official magazine of SGGSIE&T, it is completely designed by a team of students.

Drishti is not just about the magazine, instead every year Team Drishti organize certain events and programs that are a blend of artistic abilities and creativity.

Exhibition is one of the main attractions of Team Drishti, displayed in the AB halls of admin building in the institute itself. In this, exhibition, all the artworks, literary works, photographs by the students of SGGS are displayed along with some exhibits made my the team.

Drishti is all about spreading love and bonding people through art, completing 40 years gracefully.

About Purvarang


Purvarang is the official newsletter of SGGSIE&T. Purvarang has successfully released four editions and is on it's way for more.

Purvarang focuses on delivering authentic and unbiased news. Purvarang is released by a team of students. In this all the aspects that are present in any newspaper are covered, right from academics to sports.
